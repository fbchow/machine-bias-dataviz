Overall Distribution of Risk Assesment Scores
After people are booked at jail, they take the risk assessment survey and are given a pretrial risk assesment score.

1 = low risk and 10 = high risk.

Some judges use the score to inform who they release earlier from jail or the amount of bail they assign a defendant. Some judges don't use the score at all.
The Cough Effect
My theory is that at this critical halfway point in my talk, I heard a dry cough indicative of the audience's waning interest. This caused self-confidence to collapse and forced me out of my groove.

A competing theory is that I just hadn't practiced the last half of my speech as much.
